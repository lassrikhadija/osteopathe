The practitioner

Attentive listening, precise hands.

A graduate of a five-year osteopathy program, Camille has cared for patients of all ages for over twelve years — from newborns to elite athletes.

Her belief: the body has a remarkable capacity for self-regulation. The osteopath's role is to release the blockages that keep it from working freely, with gentle techniques — never abrupt.

Every session is a moment for you: we take the time to understand, explain, and give you the keys to maintain your balance day to day.

“To heal is first to listen to the body.”
